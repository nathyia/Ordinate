The Lunar Cycle

Aspect B · 4 Archetypes · 29.53-day synodic month

Each archetype governs one quarter of the synodic month — the period from New Moon to New Moon. Sunlight arrives from the right. The Pilgrim is born at the dark new moon; the Beacon at the fully lit full moon. The Adept's right half and the Mystic's left half correspond to the waxing and waning phases respectively.
